Detailed Impacts of a Geomagnetic Storm

🛰️

Satellites at Risk

Increased atmospheric drag can cause satellites to fall from orbit, and radiation can damage their sensitive electronics.

📡

Communication Disruption

Solar flares can disrupt the ionosphere, leading to radio blackouts and scrambling GPS signals, affecting navigation.

⚡

Power Grid Failure

Induced ground currents can overload electrical transformers, potentially causing widespread and long-lasting blackouts.

👨‍🚀✈️

Astronauts & Aviation

Astronauts face higher radiation risks. Polar flights are often rerouted to avoid communication loss and radiation exposure.
